About Yatri Mitra


Welcome to Yatri Mitra, your trusted travel companion for hassle-free rides. Owned and operated by Metazen Labs Pvt. Ltd., Yatri Mitra is more than just a ride-hailing app—it's a solution designed to make your daily commute and travel experiences seamless, reliable, and safe.

We understand the diverse travel needs of our users, whether it's a quick trip across town, a comfortable ride to work, or a family outing. With our easy-to-use platform, transparent pricing, and commitment to punctuality, Yatri Mitra ensures you get where you need to be, when you need to be there.




Why Choose Yatri Mitra?
• Affordable Rides: Metered fares with no hidden charges.
• Safe Journeys: Verified drivers, live ride tracking, and 24/7 support for your peace of mind.
• Convenience at Your Fingertips: Book rides in just a few taps, anytime and anywhere.
• Localized Focus: Designed to meet the specific needs of Indian travelers, blending technology with the warmth of local culture.
